Refund & Return Policy

We want you to be satisfied. Below is our refund and return policy for Zkraze.

1. When we accept returns

We accept returns only if the product is damaged, defective, or if the wrong/missed item was delivered.

2. How to request a return

Please email us at zkrazestore@gmail.com with your Order ID, a brief description, and clear photo or video proof of the damage or the missing/wrong item. Requests must be made within 7 days of delivery.

3. Items not eligible

We do not accept returns for: wrong size chosen by the customer, minor color differences, used/worn items, or sale/clearance items.

4. Refunds

After we receive and inspect the returned item (if a return is required), we will issue a refund to the original payment method. Refunds typically take 5–7 business days after approval.

5. Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled only before they are shipped. Once shipped, cancellations are not possible.

6. Contact for returns

Email: zkrazestore@gmail.com
Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka
